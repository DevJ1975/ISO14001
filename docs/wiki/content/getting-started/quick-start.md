## Before you start

You need auditor credentials and, ideally, connectivity for the first load. If the backend is not configured, choose **Continue in offline demo mode** to use seed data.

## 1. Sign in

Open the app and sign in. Check the **data-source pill** in the header reads **Live** (or **Local** in demo mode).

## 2. Confirm the audit context

On **Overview**, confirm you are looking at the correct auditee, site and audit. Check the audit status before editing records.

## 3. Open the audit

On the **Audit** tab, record the **opening meeting**: scope, criteria, methods, confidentiality and schedule.

## 4. Work the checklist

On **Fieldwork**, answer clauses one at a time:

1. Read the question and guidance.
2. Pick a result: Conform, Minor NC, Major NC, OFI or N/A.
3. Add a note and a photo where useful.
4. Tap **Log finding** on anything non-conforming.

## 5. Capture evidence

On **Evidence**, confirm your photos and notes are attached to the right clause or finding and show a **synced** badge.

## 6. Grade findings

On **Findings**, open each nonconformity, state it clearly, attach objective evidence, and (as lead auditor) set the **grade** and rationale. Open a corrective action with an owner and due date.

## 7. Complete the EMS registers

On **EMS**, record significant aspects, compliance obligations and emergency preparedness for the scope you audited.

## 8. Close and report

On **Audit**, record the **closing meeting** and conclusions. On **Report**, clear the readiness checks and, as lead auditor, **sign off**.

## 9. Sync

Tap the data-source pill to push any queued changes. The pending count should reach zero.

That's a full pass. For depth on any step, see the [Comprehensive User Guide](user-guide).
