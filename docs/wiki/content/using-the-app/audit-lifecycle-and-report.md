## Lifecycle states

An audit moves through: **planned → fieldwork → reporting → follow-up → closed**. The Audit tab shows a status stepper; only the lead auditor advances the status.

## Opening meeting

Record the opening meeting at the start of fieldwork. Confirm with the auditee:

- scope, objectives and criteria edition;
- audit methods and the schedule;
- confidentiality and information handling;
- how findings will be communicated.

## Closing meeting

At the end of fieldwork, record the closing meeting:

- present findings and agree corrective-action timelines;
- confirm the auditee's acknowledgement;
- note any diverging opinions that could not be resolved.

## Conclusions

Capture the audit conclusions (ISO 19011 6.5):

- **Overall conformity** of the EMS with the criteria.
- **EMS effectiveness opinion** — is the system achieving intended results?
- **Degree to which criteria were met**.
- **Recommendation** — recommend / conditional / not recommended for certification; or satisfactory / action required for internal audits.

## The report

The report follows ISO 19011 6.5.7 and includes objectives, scope, criteria, the team and participants, dates, findings with evidence, conclusions, the degree criteria were met, and any diverging opinions.

### Readiness checks

Before sign-off the Report screen verifies:

- all clauses answered (or marked N/A);
- nonconformities have corrective actions;
- evidence is captured and linked;
- all changes are synced.

### Sign-off

When ready, the **lead auditor** signs with an attestation. The signed report is recorded on the server with author and timestamp. Review for completeness, tone and traceability before signing.
