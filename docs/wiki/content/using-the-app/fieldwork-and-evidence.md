## Fieldwork mode

Fieldwork presents one clause at a time so you can work one-handed while walking the site.

- **Result buttons** — Conform, Minor NC, Major NC, OFI, N/A in a sticky bottom bar.
- **Progress** — the bar tracks how many clauses are answered.
- **Filters** — jump to unanswered clauses or a specific section.
- **Inline capture** — add a note or photo without leaving the clause.
- **Log finding** — raise a nonconformity directly from a non-conforming clause.

### Recommended habits

1. Confirm you are in the correct tenant, auditee and site.
2. Check the audit status before editing.
3. Sync before leaving reliable connectivity.
4. Answer every clause or mark it N/A with a reason — no silent gaps.

## Evidence

Every audit finding must rest on **objective evidence**. Each evidence record should answer three questions:

- **What** was observed?
- **Where and when** was it observed?
- **Who** captured it?

### Evidence types

| Type | Use it for |
| --- | --- |
| Photo | Conditions, labels, records, controls, locations. |
| Note | Interview responses, observations, sample references. |

### Photo tips

- Include enough surrounding context to understand the location.
- Avoid people, private information or restricted areas unless authorized.
- Retake blurry or poorly lit photos.
- Add a note when the image contains specialized equipment or site-specific terms.

### Sync badges

Each record shows its state: **queued**, **syncing** or **synced**. When offline, keep capturing normally — records sync automatically when connectivity returns.
