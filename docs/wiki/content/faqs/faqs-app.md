### What do Live, Local and Offline mean in the header?

**Live** means you are connected to the backend and changes save to the server. **Local** means the backend is not reachable or not configured, so changes are kept on the device. **Offline** means there is no connectivity; changes are queued and sync automatically when you reconnect.

### Will I lose data if I lose signal during fieldwork?

No. Everything is written to the device first and added to a sync outbox. The header shows the pending count, and queued records sync automatically when connectivity returns. You can also tap the data-source pill to sync now.

### Why can't I grade a nonconformity or sign the report?

Those actions are **lead-auditor only** and are enforced on the server. If you are signed in as an auditor, the grade and sign-off controls are hidden. Verification of effectiveness and sign-off also require connectivity.

### How do I capture photo evidence?

Open the relevant checklist item or finding, choose to take a photo, capture the condition, and add a short note explaining why it matters. Timestamp and author attach automatically (plus GPS when available). Confirm the photo is linked to the correct item and shows a *synced* badge.

### How do I install the app on my iPad?

Open the site in Safari and add it to your Home Screen. It then runs full-screen as an installed PWA and works offline in the field.

### Can I use the app without a backend?

Yes — choose **Continue in offline demo mode** at sign-in to explore the full workflow on local seed data.

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Use the theme toggle in the header. The app also follows your device's system setting by default.

### Where do I find a specific record or template?

Open the **Records & Documents Register** in this knowledge base for a master list of EMS documents and records, plus templates such as the environmental policy.
