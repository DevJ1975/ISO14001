## What counts as a compliance obligation

Compliance obligations include **legal requirements** the organization must comply with and **other requirements** it chooses to or has to comply with, such as:

- permits, licences and consents;
- environmental legislation and regulations;
- industry codes and customer requirements;
- voluntary commitments and corporate standards.

## The compliance register

| Field | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Obligation | Trade effluent consent |
| Source | Local water authority |
| Requirement (plain language) | pH 6–10; max flow 50 m³/day |
| Applicable activity | Process washdown |
| Compliance status | Compliant / At risk / Non-compliant |
| Date evaluated | 2026-04-01 |
| Evidence reference | Lab report 2026-Q1-014 |

## Evaluation of compliance (cl. 9.1.2)

Evaluate compliance at planned intervals and keep documented evidence of the result. The auditor checks both that the register is **complete and current** and that **evaluation is actually being done** with objective evidence.

## In Trainovate

Maintain this on the **EMS** tab under *Compliance obligations & evaluation of compliance*. Non-compliances should be raised as findings and driven through the corrective-action loop.
