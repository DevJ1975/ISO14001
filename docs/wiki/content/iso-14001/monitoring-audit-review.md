## Monitoring & measurement (cl. 9.1.1)

Determine **what** needs to be monitored, the **methods**, the **criteria** and **when** results are analyzed and evaluated. Use calibrated or verified equipment and keep the records as evidence of performance.

Typical metrics: energy and water use, waste and recycling rates, emissions, incident counts, permit-limit results.

## Internal audit (cl. 9.2)

Run a planned **internal audit programme** that:

- covers all parts of the EMS over time, weighted by importance and prior results;
- uses competent, **impartial** auditors (not auditing their own work);
- reports results to relevant management;
- feeds nonconformities into corrective action.

## Management review (cl. 9.3)

Top management reviews the EMS at planned intervals. Inputs include the status of actions from previous reviews, changes in issues and obligations, performance against objectives, audit results, compliance status, communications and resource needs. Outputs include decisions on improvement, changes to the EMS and resource allocation.

## In Trainovate

- **Monitoring** evidence is captured as notes/photos and referenced in registers.
- **Internal audits** are scheduled on the **Programme** tab and executed through the normal audit workflow.
- **Management review** outcomes are recorded as records (see the [Records & Documents Register](records-and-documents-register)) and feed the next programme.
